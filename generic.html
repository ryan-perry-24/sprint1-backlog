Ryan Perry

Ryan Perry is a junior studying Business Analytics & Information Systems, with a second degree in Marketing. He is also pursuing a certificate in Leadership Studies at the Tippie College of Business. Ryan is currently a student at the University of Iowa.

Originally from Downers Grove, Illinois—a suburb of Chicago—Ryan grew up a lifelong Hawkeyes fan thanks to his mother, an Iowa alum. His campus involvement includes Tau Kappa Epsilon (Treasurer), Delta Sigma Pi (Professional Business Fraternity), MoneyThink, and the Sales & Consulting Club. He is also a founder of the Applied AI Club and serves as its Vice President of Innovation. In addition, Ryan is on the Tippie Senate, the student government for the business school, where he currently serves as the Director of Case Competitions.
