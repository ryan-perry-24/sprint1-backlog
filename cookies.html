This website uses cookies to enhance your experience and help me understand how visitors engage with the content. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are and how I use them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and collect anonymous analytics data.

2. How I Use Cookies

I only use cookies through Google Analytics to collect anonymous traffic data. These cookies help me understand how users find and interact with the site, which pages are visited most often, and how I can improve user experience.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Google Analytics cookies are third-party cookies provided by Google Inc. These cookies do not identify you personally and are used only for analytical purposes.

4. Managing Cookies

You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect how some websites function, but this website will still be fully usable.

5. More Information

To learn more about Google Analytics and how your data is handled, visit Google's Privacy & Terms site.
